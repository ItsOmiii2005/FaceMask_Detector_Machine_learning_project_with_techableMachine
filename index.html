★ What Is The Product ?

The Product Is ' The Face Mask And Cough Detector (Machine Learning)'. This Product Detects That Is Anyone Were a Face Mask Or Not As Well As Is Anyone Have Cough via Detecting The Surrounding Sound.

★ What Is Need Of The Product ?

In these times of Covid-19 pandemic Wearing a mask is as essential now as ever. When Stepping Out Of Home So We Must Were a Face Mask At Every Public Place. It Will Detect Face Mask Is Put On Or Not. If Anyone Not Were a Mask Or Anyone Have Cough Then The Product Will Alarm The Device, It Will Helps To Other Persons For Keep Distance From That Person.
